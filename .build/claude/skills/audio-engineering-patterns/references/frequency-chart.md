## EQ Frequency Guide

### Sub Bass (20-60 Hz)
- **Characteristics**: Felt more than heard, rumble
- **Add**: Power, weight, physical impact
- **Cut**: Mud, rumble, headroom issues
- **Instruments**: Kick, 808, bass, synth sub

### Bass (60-250 Hz)
- **Characteristics**: Foundation, warmth, body
- **Add**: Fullness, warmth
- **Cut**: Boominess, mud, masking
- **Instruments**: Bass, kick, toms, low vocals

### Low Mids (250-500 Hz)
- **Characteristics**: Body, thickness, mud zone
- **Add**: Fullness (careful)
- **Cut**: Mud, boxiness, congestion
- **Instruments**: Guitars, vocals, snare body

### Mids (500-2000 Hz)
- **Characteristics**: Presence, honk, nasal
- **Add**: Forward presence
- **Cut**: Honk, nasal quality, harshness
- **Instruments**: Vocals, guitars, keys, snare

### Upper Mids (2-4 kHz)
- **Characteristics**: Clarity, bite, aggression
- **Add**: Definition, cut-through
- **Cut**: Harshness, listening fatigue
- **Instruments**: Vocals, guitars, attack

### Presence (4-6 kHz)
- **Characteristics**: Detail, sibilance, definition
- **Add**: Clarity, articulation
- **Cut**: Sibilance, harshness
- **Instruments**: Vocals, cymbals, acoustic guitar

### Brilliance (6-10 kHz)
- **Characteristics**: Air, shimmer, crispness
- **Add**: Brightness, sparkle
- **Cut**: Harshness, hiss
- **Instruments**: Cymbals, room mics, overheads

### Air (10-20 kHz)
- **Characteristics**: Sparkle, openness, breath
- **Add**: Air, openness, "expensive" sound
- **Cut**: Hiss, harshness, digital artifacts
- **Instruments**: Everything (subtle)

## Problem Frequencies

| Problem | Frequency | Solution |
|---------|-----------|----------|
| Rumble | <40 Hz | High-pass filter |
| Mud | 200-400 Hz | Cut 2-4 dB |
| Boxiness | 400-600 Hz | Cut 2-3 dB |
| Honk | 800-1000 Hz | Cut narrow |
| Nasal | 1-2 kHz | Cut narrow |
| Harsh | 2-4 kHz | Cut or dynamic EQ |
| Sibilance | 5-8 kHz | De-esser |
| Brittle | 8-12 kHz | Shelf cut |
