# LinkedIn Connection & Message Templates

Scripts for LinkedIn outreach, engagement, and relationship building.

## Connection Request Notes

### After Seeing Their Content

```
Hi [Name],

Your [post/article] on [specific topic] really resonated — especially
[specific point or insight]. I work in [your field] and am thinking
about similar problems.

Would love to connect and follow your thinking.

[Your Name]
```

### Shared Background

```
Hi [Name],

Fellow [university/company/program] alum here — I'm currently
[your role] at [company/doing what]. I noticed your work on
[specific project or area] and wanted to connect.

[Your Name]
```

### After a Conference or Event

```
Hi [Name],

Great seeing your [talk/panel/session] at [Event Name]. Your point
about [specific insight] was one of the highlights for me.

Would love to stay connected.

[Your Name]
```

### Mutual Connection

```
Hi [Name],

[Mutual Connection Name] and I were discussing [topic], and your
name came up as someone doing interesting work in [area].

Would love to connect and learn more about what you're building.

[Your Name]
```

### Cold Connection (No Prior Context)

```
Hi [Name],

I've been following [Company]'s work on [initiative or product]
and find the approach to [specific angle] compelling. I'm a
[your role] working on [related area].

Would be great to connect.

[Your Name]
```

## Direct Messages (After Connection)

### Opening a Conversation

```
Hi [Name],

Thanks for connecting! I've been following your posts on [topic]
and wanted to ask — [specific, thoughtful question related to
their expertise].

No rush at all, just curious to hear your perspective.

Best,
[Your Name]
```

### Informational Interview Request

```
Hi [Name],

I've been exploring a move into [field/role type] and your career
path from [A] to [B] is really inspiring. I'm currently [your
situation — brief].

Would you be open to a 15-20 minute call? I'd love to hear how
you navigated [specific transition or challenge]. Happy to work
around your schedule.

Totally understand if you're too busy — no pressure either way.

Best,
[Your Name]
```

### Asking About a Role

```
Hi [Name],

I noticed [Company] has an opening for [Role]. I've been in [your
field] for [N] years, most recently focused on [relevant area],
and the position looks like a strong match.

Would you be open to a quick chat about the team and role? I'd
also welcome any advice on what stands out in candidates for
this position.

Thanks for considering,
[Your Name]
```

### Offering Value First

```
Hi [Name],

I came across [resource/article/tool] that reminded me of your
recent post about [topic]: [link or brief description].

Thought it might be useful — let me know what you think.

Best,
[Your Name]
```

## Engagement Scripts

### Thoughtful Comment Templates

Instead of "Great post!", try:

```
Adding to your point about [X] — I've found that [related insight
from your own experience]. The distinction you make between [A]
and [B] is something most people overlook.
```

```
This matches what I saw when [brief anecdote]. One thing I'd add:
[complementary insight]. Thanks for putting this into words.
```

```
Interesting perspective on [topic]. Have you seen [related resource
or counterpoint]? I'd be curious how you'd reconcile the two views.
```

### Responding When Someone Comments on Your Post

```
Thanks, [Name] — great point about [their insight]. I've been
thinking about that too, especially in the context of [extension
of the idea]. Would love to hear more about your experience with
[related topic].
```

## Follow-Up Messages

### After an Informational Call

```
Hi [Name],

Thanks again for the conversation today. Your insights on
[specific topic] were really valuable — I especially appreciated
your advice about [specific piece of advice].

I'm going to [action you're taking based on their input]. I'll
keep you posted on how it goes.

Best,
[Your Name]
```

### Sharing a Relevant Resource (2-4 Weeks Later)

```
Hi [Name],

Came across this and thought of our conversation: [link].

[One sentence on why it connects to what you discussed.]

Hope things are going well on your end!

[Your Name]
```

### Reconnecting After Time Has Passed

```
Hi [Name],

It's been a while since we spoke — hope you're doing well!

Quick update on my end: [1-2 sentences about progress since
your last conversation].

I'd love to hear what you've been up to. Open to a catch-up
if you're ever free.

Best,
[Your Name]
```

## Profile Optimization Checklist

### Headline Formula

Instead of just your title, use:

```
[Role] | [Value you provide] | [Domain or specialization]

Examples:
- Senior Engineer | Building scalable data platforms | Previously at Stripe, Datadog
- Product Designer | Turning complex workflows into simple tools | B2B SaaS
- Freelance Writer | Making technical topics accessible | Dev tools, cloud, AI
```

### About Section Structure

```
Paragraph 1: What you do and who you help (present tense)
Paragraph 2: How you got here and what drives you (brief backstory)
Paragraph 3: What you're working on now or looking for
Paragraph 4: How to reach you / what you're open to

Include: keywords for search, specific tools or methodologies,
notable results or clients.
```

## Outreach Cadence

### Weekly Routine (30-45 minutes total)

| Day | Action | Time |
|-----|--------|------|
| Mon | Comment on 3-5 posts from target connections | 10 min |
| Tue | Send 2-3 new connection requests with notes | 5 min |
| Wed | Publish a short post or share an insight | 10 min |
| Thu | Reply to messages, send follow-ups | 10 min |
| Fri | Engage with comments on your own posts | 5 min |

### What to Avoid

- Mass connection requests with no note
- Pitching a product or service in the connection request
- Copy-pasting the same message to multiple people
- Immediately asking for a favor after connecting
- Engaging only with high-follower accounts
- Sending InMail when a connection request would work
