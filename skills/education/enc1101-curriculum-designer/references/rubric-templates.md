# Rubric Templates

## Literacy Narrative Rubric (100 points)

### Reflection & Insight (30 points)
- **27-30**: Demonstrates deep, nuanced reflection on literacy experiences; connects personal experiences to broader insights about reading/writing
- **24-26**: Shows meaningful reflection with clear connections between experiences and insights
- **21-23**: Reflects on experiences but connections to larger ideas are underdeveloped
- **18-20**: Limited reflection; mostly summary without analysis
- **0-17**: Missing or superficial reflection

### Narrative Craft (25 points)
- **23-25**: Vivid, engaging storytelling with specific details, scenes, and dialogue; clear narrative arc
- **20-22**: Effective storytelling with good details; clear structure
- **17-19**: Some narrative elements present but inconsistent; needs more specificity
- **14-16**: Reads more like summary than story; few concrete details
- **0-13**: No narrative structure or storytelling elements

### Organization (20 points)
- **18-20**: Seamless flow between scenes/ideas; purposeful structure enhances meaning
- **16-17**: Clear organization with effective transitions
- **14-15**: Some organizational logic but transitions need work
- **12-13**: Disorganized or confusing structure
- **0-11**: No discernible organization

### Style & Voice (15 points)
- **14-15**: Distinctive, engaging voice appropriate to personal narrative; varied sentence structure
- **12-13**: Appropriate voice with some variation in style
- **10-11**: Voice present but inconsistent; limited sentence variety
- **8-9**: Flat or inappropriate voice
- **0-7**: No sense of voice

### Conventions (10 points)
- **9-10**: Nearly error-free; demonstrates control of standard written English
- **8**: Few errors that don't impede understanding
- **7**: Some errors but meaning remains clear
- **5-6**: Frequent errors that sometimes impede reading
- **0-4**: Errors significantly impede comprehension

## Rhetorical Analysis Rubric (100 points)

### Thesis & Argument (25 points)
- **23-25**: Clear, specific, arguable thesis about rhetorical strategies; sustained throughout
- **20-22**: Clear thesis present; mostly sustained
- **17-19**: Thesis present but vague or not consistently supported
- **14-16**: Unclear or missing thesis
- **0-13**: No analytical argument

### Rhetorical Analysis (30 points)
- **27-30**: Sophisticated analysis of multiple rhetorical strategies (ethos, pathos, logos, audience, context); explains how strategies work together
- **24-26**: Solid analysis of rhetorical strategies with clear explanations
- **21-23**: Identifies strategies but analysis is surface-level
- **18-20**: Limited to naming strategies without analyzing effects
- **0-17**: No rhetorical analysis present

### Evidence & Support (20 points)
- **18-20**: Excellent use of textual evidence; quotes integrated smoothly with clear analysis
- **16-17**: Good evidence use; most quotes well-integrated
- **14-15**: Evidence present but integration or analysis needs work
- **12-13**: Limited evidence or dropped quotes without analysis
- **0-11**: Missing or misused evidence

### Organization (15 points)
- **14-15**: Logical structure that builds analytical argument; effective transitions
- **12-13**: Clear organization with adequate transitions
- **10-11**: Some structure but could be more logical
- **8-9**: Disorganized; hard to follow argument
- **0-7**: No organizational structure

### Conventions & Citation (10 points)
- **9-10**: Error-free; proper MLA format throughout
- **8**: Minor errors; MLA mostly correct
- **7**: Some errors; MLA inconsistent
- **5-6**: Frequent errors; citation problems
- **0-4**: Major errors impede reading; citations missing/incorrect

## Research-Based Argument Rubric (100 points)

### Thesis & Argumentation (25 points)
- **23-25**: Clear, contestable thesis; sophisticated argument with acknowledgment of counterarguments
- **20-22**: Clear thesis with solid argument; addresses opposing views
- **17-19**: Thesis present but argument underdeveloped or ignores counterarguments
- **14-16**: Weak or unclear thesis; more summary than argument
- **0-13**: No argumentative thesis

### Research & Source Integration (30 points)
- **27-30**: Excellent variety and quality of sources; seamlessly integrated with author's argument; demonstrates critical evaluation
- **24-26**: Good sources well-integrated; shows evaluation of credibility
- **21-23**: Adequate sources but integration choppy or evaluation limited
- **18-20**: Limited sources or poor integration; patchwriting present
- **0-17**: Few/no sources or source misuse

### Analysis & Development (20 points)
- **18-20**: Rich analysis connecting evidence to argument; moves beyond summary
- **16-17**: Good analysis with clear connections
- **14-15**: Some analysis but relies too heavily on summary
- **12-13**: Mostly summary; analysis underdeveloped
- **0-11**: No analysis

### Organization (15 points)
- **14-15**: Sophisticated structure serves argument; seamless transitions
- **12-13**: Clear logical structure; effective transitions
- **10-11**: Basic organization; some transition issues
- **8-9**: Hard to follow; organizational problems
- **0-7**: No clear structure

### Conventions & Citation (10 points)
- **9-10**: Error-free prose; perfect MLA/APA format
- **8**: Minor errors; citation format mostly correct
- **7**: Some errors; citation inconsistencies
- **5-6**: Frequent errors; citation problems
- **0-4**: Major errors; serious citation issues
