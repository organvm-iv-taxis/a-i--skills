# Freelance Services Agreement Template

A comprehensive contract template for freelance engagements.

---

## FREELANCE SERVICES AGREEMENT

**This Agreement** is entered into as of [DATE] ("Effective Date")

**Between:**

**Service Provider** ("Contractor"):
- Name: [YOUR LEGAL NAME]
- Business Name: [YOUR BUSINESS NAME, if applicable]
- Address: [YOUR ADDRESS]
- Email: [YOUR EMAIL]

**And:**

**Client**:
- Name/Company: [CLIENT NAME]
- Address: [CLIENT ADDRESS]
- Email: [CLIENT EMAIL]
- Contact Person: [NAME, if company]

### 3.1 Fees

**Payment Schedule:**

| Milestone | Amount | Due Date |
|-----------|--------|----------|

## 4. REVISIONS

### 4.1 Included Revisions

This agreement includes [NUMBER] rounds of revisions per deliverable.

**Revision**: Modifications to work within the original scope.

### 4.2 Additional Revisions

Revisions beyond the included rounds will be billed at:
$[RATE] per hour

### 4.3 Revision Requests

- Revision requests must be consolidated and submitted in writing
- Contractor will provide revised work within [X] business days
- Client has [X] business days to review each round

---

## 5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

### 5.1 Ownership Transfer

Upon receipt of **final payment in full**, Contractor assigns to Client all rights, title, and interest in the final deliverables.

### 5.2 Pre-Existing Materials

Contractor retains ownership of:
- Pre-existing tools, libraries, and frameworks
- Generalized knowledge and techniques

Client receives a perpetual, non-exclusive license to use these as incorporated in deliverables.

### 5.3 Portfolio Rights

Contractor may display the work in portfolio and marketing materials unless otherwise agreed in writing.

---

## 6. CONFIDENTIALITY

### 6.1 Confidential Information

Both parties agree to keep confidential:
- Business strategies and plans
- Financial information
- Proprietary processes
- Client lists and data
- Trade secrets

### 6.2 Exceptions

Confidentiality does not apply to information that:
- Becomes publicly available (not through breach)
- Was known prior to disclosure
- Is independently developed
- Is required by law to be disclosed

### 6.3 Duration

Confidentiality obligations survive for [2] years after project completion.

---

## 7. TERMINATION

### 7.1 Termination by Client

Client may terminate this agreement with [14] days written notice.

Upon termination, Client agrees to pay:
- All work completed to date
- [25]% of remaining project value (kill fee)
- Any approved expenses

### 7.2 Termination by Contractor

Contractor may terminate with [14] days written notice if:
- Payment is more than [30] days overdue
- Client materially breaches this agreement
- Circumstances prevent completion

### 7.3 Deliverables on Termination

Upon termination and final payment:
- All completed work transfers to Client
- Work-in-progress may be delivered at Contractor's discretion

---

## 8. WARRANTIES AND LIABILITY

### 8.1 Contractor Warranties

Contractor warrants that:
- Work will be original or properly licensed
- Contractor has authority to enter this agreement
- Work will substantially conform to specifications

### 8.2 Limitation of Liability

**Total liability is limited to the fees paid under this agreement.**

Neither party is liable for:
- Indirect, incidental, or consequential damages
- Lost profits or data
- Damages from third-party claims

### 8.3 Client Warranties

Client warrants that:
- Materials provided are properly licensed
- Client has authority to enter this agreement
- Information provided is accurate

---

## 9. GENERAL PROVISIONS

### 9.1 Independent Contractor

Contractor is an independent contractor, not an employee. Contractor is responsible for own taxes, insurance, and benefits.

### 9.2 Non-Solicitation

Client agrees not to solicit or hire Contractor's subcontractors for [12] months after project completion.

### 9.3 Force Majeure

Neither party is liable for delays due to circumstances beyond reasonable control.

### 9.4 Amendments

Modifications require written agreement signed by both parties.

### 9.5 Governing Law

This agreement is governed by the laws of [STATE/COUNTRY].

### 9.6 Dispute Resolution

Disputes will be resolved through:
1. Good faith negotiation
2. Mediation in [LOCATION]
3. Binding arbitration if mediation fails

### 9.7 Entire Agreement

This document constitutes the entire agreement and supersedes all prior discussions.

## Usage Notes

### Customize These Sections:
- Scope of work (Section 1)
- Timeline and milestones (Section 2)
- Compensation amounts (Section 3)
- Number of revisions (Section 4)
- Kill fee percentage (Section 7.1)
- Governing law location (Section 9.5)

### Consider Adding For Complex Projects:
- NDA as separate document
- Detailed acceptance criteria
- Testing and QA process
- Warranty period terms
- Source code escrow

### Legal Note:
This template provides a starting point. Have an attorney review contracts for significant engagements or if you have specific legal concerns.
