# Interview Question Bank

100+ common interview questions organized by category.

## Opening Questions

### About You
1. Tell me about yourself.
2. Walk me through your resume.
3. Why are you interested in this role?
4. Why are you leaving your current position?
5. What do you know about our company?
6. Why do you want to work here?
7. Where do you see yourself in 5 years?
8. What are you looking for in your next role?

---

## Behavioral Questions

### Leadership

9. Tell me about a time you led a team.
10. Describe a situation where you had to lead without formal authority.
11. Give an example of when you had to make a difficult decision.
12. Tell me about a time you delegated effectively.
13. Describe how you've mentored or developed someone.
14. Tell me about a time you had to motivate a struggling team.
15. When have you taken charge in an unstructured situation?
16. Describe a time you had to lead through change.

### Conflict & Difficult Situations

17. Tell me about a conflict with a coworker and how you resolved it.
18. Describe a time you disagreed with your manager.
19. How have you handled a difficult stakeholder?
20. Tell me about a time you received critical feedback.
21. Describe a situation where you had to deliver bad news.
22. How have you dealt with a team member not pulling their weight?
23. Tell me about navigating office politics.
24. Describe handling a customer or client complaint.

### Failure & Learning

25. Tell me about a time you failed.
26. Describe a mistake you made and what you learned.
27. When did a project you led not go as planned?
28. Tell me about a time you missed a deadline.
29. Describe a goal you didn't achieve.
30. When have you had to admit you were wrong?
31. Tell me about receiving feedback that was hard to hear.
32. Describe a time you had to change your approach.

### Achievement & Success

33. What's your greatest professional achievement?
34. Tell me about a time you exceeded expectations.
35. Describe your biggest impact at your current/last job.
36. When have you gone above and beyond?
37. Tell me about a goal you achieved.
38. Describe a project you're particularly proud of.
39. When did you deliver something ahead of schedule?
40. Tell me about a time you received recognition.

### Problem-Solving

41. Describe how you approach a new problem.
42. Tell me about a complex problem you solved.
43. When have you had to make a decision with incomplete information?
44. Describe a time you identified a problem before others did.
45. Tell me about an innovative solution you developed.
46. How have you handled ambiguity?
47. Describe debugging a particularly difficult issue.
48. When did you have to think creatively to solve something?

### Collaboration & Teamwork

49. Describe your ideal team environment.
50. Tell me about working with a cross-functional team.
51. How have you built relationships with new teammates?
52. Describe a time you had to rely on others to get work done.
53. Tell me about collaborating with a remote team.
54. When have you helped a colleague succeed?
55. Describe working with someone very different from you.
56. How do you handle working with people you don't like?

### Initiative & Drive

57. Tell me about a time you took initiative.
58. Describe a project you started on your own.
59. When have you pushed for something you believed in?
60. Tell me about going beyond your job description.
61. Describe learning something new for a project.
62. When have you identified an opportunity others missed?
63. Tell me about proposing a new idea or process.
64. Describe taking a calculated risk.

### Communication

65. Describe presenting to senior leadership.
66. Tell me about explaining something technical to a non-technical audience.
67. When have you had to persuade someone?
68. Describe giving constructive feedback.
69. Tell me about writing an important document or proposal.
70. How have you handled a miscommunication?
71. Describe facilitating a difficult meeting.
72. When did you have to adjust your communication style?

## Role-Specific Questions

### For Managers

73. How do you approach hiring?
74. Describe your management style.
75. How do you give feedback to your team?
76. Tell me about performance managing someone out.
77. How do you handle a high performer who's difficult to work with?
78. Describe building a team from scratch.
79. How do you prioritize your team's work?
80. Tell me about developing your direct reports.

### For Individual Contributors

81. How do you prioritize your work?
82. Describe managing multiple competing deadlines.
83. How do you stay current in your field?
84. Tell me about owning a project end-to-end.
85. How do you handle interruptions?
86. Describe working independently.
87. When have you disagreed with a decision but committed anyway?
88. How do you measure your own success?

### Technical/Role-Specific

89. Walk me through a technical project you led.
90. Describe your approach to [specific skill].
91. How do you stay updated on [industry/technology]?
92. Tell me about a technical trade-off you made.
93. Describe your debugging/troubleshooting process.
94. How do you ensure quality in your work?
95. Tell me about documentation you've created.
96. Describe working within technical constraints.

## Challenging Questions

### Weaknesses & Growth Areas

97. What's your greatest weakness?
98. What feedback do you consistently receive?
99. What are you working to improve?
100. What would your current manager say about your areas for growth?

### Gaps & Concerns

101. Why the gap in your employment?
102. Why did you leave [job] after only [short time]?
103. Why are you looking to change industries?
104. What makes you qualified given you haven't done [X]?
105. Why should we hire you over someone with more experience?

### Hypotheticals

106. What would you do in your first 90 days?
107. How would you approach [specific scenario]?
108. If you had to choose between [A] and [B], which would you pick?
109. How would you handle a situation where [conflict scenario]?
110. What would you do if you disagreed with a company decision?

---

## Questions to Ask Them

### About the Role

111. What does success look like in the first 90 days?
112. What are the biggest challenges facing this role?
113. How is performance measured?
114. What's the team's biggest priority right now?
115. What would a typical week look like?

### About the Team

116. Can you tell me about the team I'd be working with?
117. How does the team collaborate?
118. What's the team's working style?
119. How does the team handle disagreements?
120. Who would I be working with most closely?

### About Growth

121. What growth opportunities exist in this role?
122. How have people in this role progressed?
123. What learning and development resources are available?
124. How does the company support career development?
125. What would the next step be from this role?

### About Culture

126. How would you describe the culture?
127. What do you enjoy most about working here?
128. How does the team celebrate wins?
129. How does the company handle failure?
130. What's the work-life balance like?

### Closing

131. Is there anything about my background that gives you pause?
132. What are the next steps in the process?
133. Is there anything else I can clarify?
134. When can I expect to hear back?
135. Is there anything else you'd like to know about me?
