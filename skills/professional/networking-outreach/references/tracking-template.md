## Status Stages

### Relationship Lifecycle

```
Researching → Outreach Sent → Replied → Scheduled →
Completed → Follow-Up Due → Warm Contact
```

| Status | Definition | Next Action |
|--------|------------|-------------|
| Researching | Gathering info before outreach | Prepare personalized email |
| Outreach Sent | Initial email sent, awaiting reply | Wait 4-5 days, then bump |
| Bumped | Follow-up sent | Wait 7-10 days, then final |
| Final Sent | Last follow-up sent | Move to dormant if no reply |
| Replied | They responded | Respond/schedule within 24hr |
| Scheduled | Meeting booked | Prepare, attend, follow up |
| Completed | Meeting done, thank you sent | Add to nurture list |
| Follow-Up Due | Time for a check-in | Send value or update |
| Warm Contact | Active relationship | Maintain with periodic touch |
| Dormant | No recent activity | Consider reactivation |

## Weekly Workflow

### Monday: Plan

```
[ ] Review upcoming follow-ups due this week
[ ] Identify 3-5 new people to reach out to
[ ] Research new contacts (15-20 min each)
```

### Tuesday-Thursday: Execute

```
[ ] Send 2-3 new outreach emails per day
[ ] Send follow-ups to non-responders (day 4-5)
[ ] Respond to any replies within 24 hours
[ ] Prepare for scheduled calls
```

### Friday: Review

```
[ ] Update tracker with week's activity
[ ] Move contacts to appropriate statuses
[ ] Plan next week's outreach targets
[ ] Send any thank-you notes for completed calls
```

## Nurture Schedule

### Maintain Warm Contacts

| Relationship Strength | Touch Frequency | Touch Type |
|-----------------------|-----------------|------------|
| Strong (key contacts) | Every 4-6 weeks | Personal note, value share |
| Medium (good rapport) | Every 2-3 months | Article share, check-in |
| Light (met once) | Every 4-6 months | Congratulate on news |

### Touch Ideas

| Type | Example | When to Use |
|------|---------|-------------|
| Value share | "Thought of you when I saw this article..." | When you find relevant content |
| Congrats | "Saw the news about X—congrats!" | Promotions, company news, wins |
| Check-in | "It's been a while—how are things?" | Periodic maintenance |
| Introduction | "I'd love to connect you with..." | When you can add value |
| Update | "Wanted to share—I [did thing they advised]" | After taking their advice |

## Search & Targeting

### Finding New Contacts

#### By Goal

| Goal | Where to Look |
|------|---------------|
| Job at specific company | LinkedIn (employees), Alumni networks |
| Industry learning | Podcasts, Conferences, Twitter/X |
| Expertise in topic | Authors, Speakers, Course instructors |
| General networking | Events, Online communities, Alumni |

#### Research Checklist

Before reaching out to someone new:

- [ ] LinkedIn profile reviewed
- [ ] Recent posts/content noted
- [ ] Mutual connections identified
- [ ] Company/team context understood
- [ ] Specific reason to connect identified
- [ ] Personalized talking point prepared

## Meeting Preparation

### Pre-Call Checklist

```
[ ] Review their LinkedIn/background (10 min)
[ ] Review any previous interactions
[ ] Prepare 5-7 questions
[ ] Have your "tell me about yourself" ready
[ ] Know what you want to learn
[ ] Calendar their time zone correctly
[ ] Test video/audio if virtual
```

### Question Template

```
Career Path:
- How did you get into [field]?
- What led you from [A] to [B]?

Role Insight:
- What does a typical week look like?
- What surprised you about this role?

Industry/Company:
- What trends are you watching?
- What's the culture really like?

Advice:
- What would you do differently?
- What should I be learning?

Closing:
- Who else should I talk to?
- How can I be helpful to you?
```

### Post-Call Actions

```
[ ] Send thank-you within 24 hours
[ ] Connect on LinkedIn (if not already)
[ ] Add notes to tracker
[ ] Schedule any promised follow-ups
[ ] Reach out to any referrals they gave
[ ] Set reminder for next touch point
```
